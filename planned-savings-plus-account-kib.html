Planned Savings PLUS Account

Start setting aside a small amount every month and save towards your financial goals & future dreams.

APPLY NOW

Interest–bearing and contract–based savings account

Customer is allowed to make additional deposit any time during the contract period and as many times as the customer wishes.

Key Benefits

  • Reach financial goal
  • High interest rate
  • Flexibility in deposit term from 3 months to 36 months
  • Making additional deposit anytime
  • High security for your cash
  • Interest is calculated daily and paid on maturity date

Requirement

For Individual

  • A legal person, both Cambodian and Foreigner
  • Identification Documents such as ID Card, Passport and/or other document

For Corporate or Financial Institution

  • An entity that is legally registered under the Cambodian’s laws and regulations
  • Business Registration Certificate, Memorandum and Articles of Association, and/or Letter of Authorization
