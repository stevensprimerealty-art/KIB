Start setting aside a small amount every month and save towards your financial goals & future dreams with Planned Savings Account (PSA). You can make regular deposits with a specific amount and period for competitive interest rates.

Key Benefits

  • Reach your finance goals and future dreams
  • Flexibility in deposit term from 3 months to 36 months
  • Affordable monthly deposit with amount as low as KHR 40,000 or USD 10
  • High interest rate
  • Choose the most comfortable deposit term and amount

Requirement

For Individual

  • A legal person, both Cambodian and Foreigner
  • Identification Documents such as ID Card, Passport and/or other document

For Corporate or Financial Institution

  • An entity that is legally registered under the Cambodian’s laws and regulations
  • Business Registration Certificate, Memorandum and Articles of Association, and/or Letter of Authorization or Board Resolution as well as other documents (if available)
