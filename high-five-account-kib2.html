Save Smart and Earn More with High Five Savings Account!

High Five Savings Account is a saving account that offer multi-high interest rate on different amount of savings balance. HFA also allows customers to enjoy extra benefits such as getting access to mobile banking service, fund transfer, and other banking services.

Key Benefits

  • High security for your cash
  • Receive high interest rate
  • Deposit or withdrawal anytime
  • Open an account without service charge
  • Interest is calculated daily and paid monthly
  • Easy to make financial and non-financial transaction via KIB mobile banking

Requirements for Individuals only

A legal person, both Cambodian and Foreigner

Identification documents such as ID Card, Passport and/or other documents

Conditions

Currency
KHR and USD

Initial Deposit
Not Require (Zero initial deposit)

Minimum Ongoing Balance
KHR 20,000 or USD 5

Eligibility
Individual Customer only

Annual Interest Rate
5% and 1%

Notes:

  • Interest Rate 5% per annum will be applied for account balance ≤$555 or KHR 2,220,000.
  • For excess balance above this, 1% per annum applies.
