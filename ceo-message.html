Message from SOHN CHEOL SU , CEO

Dear Valued Customers,

I am honored and excited to be appointed as the CEO of Korea International Banking (Cambodia) Plc. Joining Korea International Banking is a privilege, and I am eager to contribute to the continued success of the bank in the Cambodian market.

Throughout my career at Korea International Banking, I have consistently focused on understanding and meeting the evolving financial needs of our customers. I believe in fostering strong customer relationships built on trust, transparency, and personalized services.

As a CEO, I am committed to leading Korea International Banking (Cambodia) Plc. towards new heights of excellence. We will leverage innovation, enhance our customer service, and strengthen our partnerships to provide our customers with the smartest and best possible financial solutions. We will continue to support the growth and development of the Cambodian economy while upholding the highest standards of corporate responsibility.

I look forward to working closely with our dedicated team to serve our valued customers and the whole Cambodian community as we embark on this exciting new chapter.

Sincerely,

Mr. CHEOL SU SOHN
Chief Executive Officer
Korea International Banking (Cambodia) Plc.
