Fund Transfer

KIB Bank is one of the Fund Transfer members. KIB Bank has integrated Fund Transfer in KIB Bank Mobile Cambodia which customers are able to access their Fund Transfer Account easily.

Key Benefits

  • Fund Transfer Account Registration on KIB Bank Mobile App
  • Fund transfer from KIB Bank Hope Saving account to Fund Transfer member’s current/saving account or receive fund from Fund Transfer member via KIB Bank Mobile with free of charge until 31 December 2022
  • Deposit fund to Fund Transfer members
  • Make payment via QR
  • Create New Recovery QR Code
  • Fund Transfer Account Restore Access on KIB Bank Mobile App
  • View account balance & transaction history of Fund Transfer on KIB Bank Mobile App
  • View Fund Transfer account information on KIB Bank Mobile App

Registration

✔ Registration is made through NBC’s Fund Transfer App (Generic App). Customer shall perform self-registration by choosing KIB Bank Mobile in the bank list.
✔ The registration can also be made through KIB Bank Mobile Banking, KIB Bank Mobile App.

Eligible

Customers are required to have Hope Saving Account with KIB Bank.

Currency

KHR and USD

Maximum Amount Transfer

1. Transfer funds transaction between Fund Transfer wallet and Fund Transfer wallet can transfer up to 40,000,000 Riels or 10,000 USD per day.

2. Transfer funds transaction between KIB Bank account and Fund Transfer member’s Bank account can transfer up to 200,000,000 Riels or 50,000 USD per day.
