Community Deposit Support (CDS)

Make your saving worth for you and others. Deposit with CDS to get higher interest rate and chance to help people in need.

Bigger Rate, Biggest Heart

Community Deposit Support (CDS) is a fixed-term deposit account with terms ranging from three to twelve months that is embedded in charity or social contribution. CDS allows customers to choose their preferred charity organizations that are KIB partners. KIB will cover the charity fund which will be donated to a charity organization's account in the customer's name.

Key Benefits

  • High interest rate
  • Interest is calculated daily and paid monthly or on maturity date
  • Participation in charity act for society
  • High security for your cash
