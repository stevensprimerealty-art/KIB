Best Junior Account

Smart savings choice for your kids!

Best Junior Account is new initiative savings account designed to allow child aged from 10 to 17 years old to experience the banking services by allowing them with some financial transactions under the control of their Parents/Guardian.

Key Benefits

  • Get high interest rate
  • Promote children’s financial literacy
  • Help children experience real banking services through KIB mobile
  • Help children understand how money works in daily lifestyle
  • Assist parent/guardian fully monitor and control their children’s habit

Requirements

For Child

  • Cambodian and foreigner whose age is between 10 to 17 years old
  • Valid documents (Birth Certificate / ID / Passport)

For Parents/Guardian

  • At least 18 years old with valid ID
  • Relationship document required

Conditions

Currency: KHR and USD

Initial Deposit: Not Required

Minimum Balance: Not Required

Interest Payment: Posted monthly

Eligibility: Individual Customer (10–17 years old)

How To Apply

  • Visit nearest KIB branch
  • Contact KIB support for assistance
