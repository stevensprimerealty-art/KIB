SME Loan

A loan for Small and Medium Enterprises.

APPLY NOW

Grow your business with KIB Bank's SME Loan!

Our SME loans provide essential financial support for small and medium-sized businesses, available to both individual and corporate clients.

Loan purposes:

  • For start-up new business (it is applicable for applicant having existing sources of income to secure the loan repayment);
  • For capital expenditure / investment;
  • For working capital (it is applicable for applicants who need cash to support short term payment or need a short term financing for specific business activity);
  • For business expansion / diversification;
  • For agriculture (agricultural machinery / production);
  • For refinancing loan from other FIs to serve any item above.

Benefits:

  • Big loan size suitable for all types of business;
  • Competitive interest rate;
  • Loan term up to 120 months;
  • Fast approval process.

Conditions

  • Third party guarantee;
  • Bank Guarantee.
