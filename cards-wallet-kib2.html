KIB ATM Card

provides customers convenient, fast and sure way to perform financial transactions. KIB’s Card holder can use his/her ATM card to perform transactions for such as cash withdrawal, fund transfer, purchase, balance inquiry and mini-statement, and other financial transactions through KIB’s ATM and other ATM/POS of all CSS’s members anywhere, anytime throughout Cambodia.

Key Benefits

  • Get ATM Card for free of charge
  • Convenient and secure without carrying large amount of cash
  • Service is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week
  • Can be operated through all ATM or POS with “CSS” Logo
  • Free of charge for all transactions though KIB ATM
  • One card can be operated for up to 03 (three) different Savings Accounts
